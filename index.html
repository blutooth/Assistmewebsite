Leadership

Marie Erete

Principal Solicitor & Founder

"My goal is to provide the kind of legal support I would want for my own family: honest, expert, and deeply empathetic."

Marie Erete is a solicitor advocate with higher rights of audience who has practiced law for over three decades. She began her legal career at Linklaters and Paines. Her cases have included representing the President of Nigeria in the UK, as well as other international clients and their families, bringing a global perspective to her practice.

Marie founded AssistMe Solicitors to bridge the gap between high-level legal expertise and the ordinary person. She specializes in complex child arrangement cases and high-net-worth financial settlements, combining her extensive experience with a compassionate, client-focused approach.
