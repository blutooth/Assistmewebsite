Leadership

Marie Erete

Principal Solicitor & Founder

With over 15 years of dedicated practice in family law, Marie Erete founded AssistMe Solicitors with a clear vision: to provide compassionate, expert legal guidance to families during their most challenging moments.

Marie's approach combines rigorous legal expertise with genuine empathy. Having worked with hundreds of families across the United Kingdom, she understands that every case represents real people navigating difficult life transitions.

Her practice philosophy centres on clear communication, transparent pricing, and achieving the best possible outcomes for her clients—particularly when children's welfare is at stake.
